Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 March 2025

1. Introduction

STS Companion ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address.

Study data: Your study progress, review question answers, personal notes, text highlights, and app settings are stored to sync across sessions.

Subscription data: Your subscription status is managed through RevenueCat, which communicates with Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not directly process or store payment card details.

Usage data: We may collect basic usage analytics (e.g. which features are used) to improve the App. This data is anonymised and cannot identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain your account and study progress
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To improve the App's features and performance

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for secure account sign-in
  • Firebase Firestore — for storing your study data
  • RevenueCat — for subscription and purchase management

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the App.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure). We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Export your study data upon request
  • Withdraw consent by deleting your account at any time

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at stscompanionapp@gmail.com.
